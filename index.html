I am a PhD student in the College of Engineering at Northeastern University. I am a member of MAGICS Lab (Multi AGent Intelligent Complex Systems) , and my advisor is Babak Heydari.

My research interests are at the intersection of applied artificial intelligence and social science. Currently, my focus is on analyzing the causal effect of system-level treatments on users' interaction patterns in digital platforms: who is more affected by ChatGPT, what type of users, and why?
